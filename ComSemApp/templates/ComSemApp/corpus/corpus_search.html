Offset determines how many spaces away the two selected words can be from each other. For example, if you want to see "the dog" you would type "the" into the first search box and "dog" into the second search box, with an offset of 1.

The offset indicates "up to and including," so for an offset value of 4, two words may be shown together between 1 and 4 spaces away. Negative values mean the second word will come first in the sentences.
